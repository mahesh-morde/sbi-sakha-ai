A Day in the Life of SAKHA

Watch how the AI ecosystem orchestrates Rahul's financial journey.

Account Opening

Rahul wants to open an account. Aarambh welcomes him in Hindi, scans his Aadhar card via WhatsApp, and sets up his account in 3 minutes.

Salary Credited

First salary hits the account. Mitra detects the deposit and proactively congratulates him, suggesting he allocate 20% to savings.

Goal Planning

Rahul mentions he wants to buy a car. Nivesh instantly calculates the required SIP amount and sets up an auto-debit mutual fund plan.

Home Loan

Two years later, Rahul asks about houses. Pragati reviews his transaction history, instantly pre-approves an EMI, and initiates the loan.

Fraud Detected

An anomalous overseas transaction occurs. Suraksha instantly freezes the card and triggers a voice call to Rahul to confirm the action.
